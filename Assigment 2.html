Recipe of the Day: Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs,chees,pancetta, and pepper. It's Simple ,delicious and perfect for any accasion

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated cheeses.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix witn pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the egg and Chees mixture.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
